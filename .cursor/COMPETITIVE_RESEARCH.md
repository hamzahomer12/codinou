# Codinou — Competitive landscape (reference)

Use the **`competitive-research`** agent (`.cursor/agents/competitive-research.md`) or skill **codinou-competitive-research** to refresh this file.

## Executive summary

- French Odoo integrators (Apik, Kodo, YesWeDoo, Pure Illusion) lead with **tiered ERP packages**, training, migration, and e-commerce sync — rarely pure “website only.”
- AI + automation players (e.g. Fanto) bundle **Odoo + AI** with measurable outcomes (time saved, automation %).
- Codinou’s edge: **one studio** for websites, web apps, AI, e-commerce, full digital programs, and Odoo — with deliverable-based packages and bilingual copy.

## Competitor patterns to mirror (ethically)

| Pattern | Codinou implementation |
|---------|-------------------------|
| 3 tiers per line | 18 packages across 6 services (`lib/services-data.ts`) |
| Timeline + “best for” | On each `PackageCard` |
| Quote after discovery | CTAs → `/contact`, no fake prices |
| ERP + web + shop | Odoo + e-commerce + digital experience sections |

## When to re-run research

- Adding pricing pages
- Entering a new geography
- New service line (e.g. mobile apps only)
