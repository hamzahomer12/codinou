# Codinou pricing research (2025–2026)

## Positioning

Codinou = creative web studio (not large agency, not cheapest freelance). Three services only:

1. Websites
2. Web applications
3. AI solutions (automations & agents)

## Market benchmarks used

| Segment | Market range (EUR) | Source |
|---------|-------------------|--------|
| Landing page | 300 – 1,500 (freelance), 1,000 – 5,000 (agency) | na-web.fr, WebCraftDev |
| Business website | 800 – 5,000 (freelance), 3,000 – 15,000 (agency) | na-web.fr |
| Web app MVP | 5,000 – 25,000 (freelance), 20,000 – 60,000 (agency) | na-web.fr, EID Lab |
| AI workflow | ~1,500 – 4,500 setup | Novasoft, Arsum |
| AI agent | ~3,500 – 9,000 setup | Novasoft, ADAI |
